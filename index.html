Welcome to ICEEICT 2021

The 5th International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Information & Communication Technology (ICEEICT 2021) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, scholars to exchange and share their experiences, research results about all aspects of Electrical Engineering, ICT and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. The conference will feature a comprehensive technical program offering numerous technical sessions with papers showcasing the latest technologies, applications and services. In addition, the conference program includes workshops, tutorials, keynote talks from renowned academics, panel discussions, poster presentations, and a large exhibition. The conference is organized by the Electrical, Electronic and Communication Engineering (EECE) Department of Military Institute of   Science and Technology (MIST), Mirpur, Dhaka.

The best paper, poster, and presentation awards will be announced during the conference based on the recommendations of reviewers and the program committee. All the accepted and presented papers in the ICEEICT 2021 will be submitted for inclusion into the digital library of IEEE Xplore.
